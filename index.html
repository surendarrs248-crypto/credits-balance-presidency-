Course Equivalence Mapping
Map old-batch / old-curriculum subjects to new-batch equivalents — essential when a year-back or sem-back student rejoins a revised syllabus
ℹ️
How course equivalence works
When the curriculum changes between batches, a subject a student already cleared (old code) may be replaced by a renamed/restructured subject (new code) in the revised syllabus. Map them here so credit already earned is correctly carried forward instead of being asked to repeat the course.

Semester Load Balancer
Upload or select current-semester and backlog (previous-semester) courses side by side to balance combined credit load
⚖️
How the balancer works
Upload (or pick manually) the courses planned for the current semester and the backlog / previous-semester courses still pending. The balancer adds up both credit loads, compares the combined total against your configured maximum per-semester limit, and shows the equivalence gap if any of the backlog subjects have an old → new curriculum mapping.
